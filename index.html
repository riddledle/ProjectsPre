HUA REN SHUO

华人说

1st Nov 2016

华人说是一款针对海外华人的新闻社区类App，旨在打造全球华人聚集的资讯平台，在产品设计上借鉴了海外产品Reddit官方App，内容展示的方式更加符合国人阅读习惯，通过快速上线试错的方式抢占海外市场。

这款产品能够诞生，前期我们做了许多准备工作，包括分析Reddit、选择技术框架、规划产品、挑选名称与口号等。通过不断推动一个想法，使之从空中聚焦和落地，并且做到快速上线，是很刺激的体验。

HUA REN SHUOis a PGC App for Chinese oversea which is aimed at becoming an information platform gathering global Chinese. It gained design principles from Reddit but information is displayed in a way which is more familiar to Chinese. This is a rapid product for trials and errors.

We made lots of preparations for this product during its prophase including researching Reddit, choosing frameworks, designing product and picking its name&slogan. To some degree, it's exciting to promote an idea and bring it to reality.

YIDAO TENNIS

易道网球

1st Nov 2016

易道网球是一款主营教练课程和预订场地服务的运动健身产品，以微信公众号为载体，将目前易道体育线下运营的会员套餐系统线上化，让用户可在微信上进行会员套餐购买、课程预约、课程管理、上课签到等行为，运营人员可在运营后台管理课程及会员，从而提高易道体育目前的运营效率及营收。

刚开始输出产品需求文档和做竞品分析的时候自己还相当青涩，还好能够不断磨练改进，从而对每一份文档都能够怀抱敬重和负责的态度。

Yidao tennisis a Weixin project which serves the sports&fitness market and offers reservations for teaching courses and venues. It turns the VIP system from off-line to on-line, making the prachase for VIP service and reservation for courses conducted in WeiXin possible, administrators can also perform admistrations over users resulting in an improvement in efficiency.

I'm grateful for the adversity during my learning process of writing product requirement document and competitor analysis which brought me respect for each document ever after.

MO SHU XIONG

魔术熊

22nd Aug 2016

魔术熊外送：县城第一家O2O外送平台，致力于发展三四线城市移动互联网外送服务，提供网上订餐、准时外送、农产品、土特产交易；以内蒙古五个县城为试点，正在积极扩张。

产品的全部内容包含宣传推广页、手机客户端、商家管理后台共三部分，宣传推广页包含官方网站及其相关信息页，手机客户端上架App Store和安卓应用市场，商家管理后台供商户和超级管理员管理商铺、商品和财务。

这个项目采用的是团队协作开发和远程合作的方式，尽管协调各部门、梳理产品逻辑、产品测试与上线等事情让我感到了忙碌但同时我也非常开心。

MO-SHU-XIONG delivery: The first O2O delivery platform in town, making efforts to enlarge the delivery service including making orders online, on-time deliveries, farm produce, etc in towns especially in Nei Meng Gu.

Promotions,mobile clientand administrationmake up the whole product. Promotions are composed of the official site and its relations, the mobile client can be obtained from App Store and Android stores, administration provides management over stores, goods and finance.

This project required teamworks and remote-coorperation, i did feel a little busy during the process of coordinating different departments, organizing product logics and testing but also very pleased.

INTERNET FINANCIAL PLATFORM

互联网金融大数据监管平台

22nd Jul 2016

数控金融监管平台针对贵阳市大数据建设和金融业发展现状而设计创建，利用数据可视化工具（Echarts）通过图表形式全方位，多方面地展示数据，从而通过大数据技术对个人和公司进行评判，以防范金融风险。

这是我的第一个项目，通宵的情形还历历在目，但是能够从0到1完成一个项目的感受非常美妙，Echarts也是一款表现力很强的数据工具。这次经历使我深深被人类的潜力所感动，那是无限的东西，感受非常深刻。

Internet Financial Platformis designed and constructed for the Big Data career and financial business in Guiyang province. It uses the data visualization tool - Echarts to display data in all directions and aspects in the form of various charts, in order to evaluate individuals and enterprises, preventing them from financial risks.

This is my very first project ever in my career, there were days when i stayed up all night. However the fantastic taste of experiencing a 0 to 1 project is rewarding.Echarts is also a high-performance tool. I was deeply impressed by the infinite potential of a simple human being in many moments.

IN MY JNU

掌上小暨微信平台

31st Mar 2016

掌上小暨定位于暨南大学，是集校园教学、工作和生活服务功能于一体的线上结构性服务系统平台，其目的在于整合校园各类信息与服务，通过O2O运营模式为暨南大学广大师生提供一个全方位、广覆盖、高效便捷的网络综合服务环境。

掌上小暨自2016年3月创立至今，粉丝人数达13000人，主要活跃于暨南大学南校区，覆盖南校区90%以上学生，最近30天图文阅读总次数达244701次，累计190272人阅读，南校区自媒体平台第一。

我因为契机加入了该平台并伴随其成长发展，期间参与过商业合作、活动运营和内容运营，为平台积累了大量用户。校园自媒体的运营经历对我来说是大学期间一次不错的体验。

InMyJnuis located in JNU which is an online service platform integrating teaching affairs and life service in the campus. It aims at providing a wide-spread comprehensive internet environment for teachers and students by running the O2O operating model.

The platform has gained over 13000 fans since its foundation in 2016.3 covering over 90% of the students in south campus, the total views in the latest month has achieved 244701 times by 190272 people, winning the first place in south campus.

I became part of the operating team by chance. I've taken part in its commercial cooperations, event operations and content operations and won many fans for the platform. This is genuinely a meaningful experience for me during my campus life.
