通过不断推动一个想法，使之从空中聚焦和落地，并且做到快速上线，我觉得是很刺激的体验。

MO SHU XIONG

魔树熊

22nd Aug 2016

魔树熊外送：县城第一家O2O外送平台，致力于发展三四线城市移动互联网外送服务，提供网上订餐、准时外送、农产品、土特产交易；以内蒙古五个县城为试点，正在积极扩张。

产品的全部内容包含宣传推广页、手机客户端、商家管理后台共三部分，宣传推广页包含官方网站及其相关信息页，手机客户端上架App Store和安卓应用市场，商家管理后台供商户和超级管理员管理商铺、商品和财务。前端技术框架我们选择的是跨平台纯原生开发工具Deviceone，利用Hybrid开发技术（混合开发技术），在确保用户体验良好的前提下完美实现所有功能，并在协调沟通下解决处理好了兼容性问题。

这个项目采用的是团队协作开发和远程合作的方式，尽管协调各部门、梳理产品逻辑、产品测试与上线等事情让我感到了忙碌但同时我也非常开心。

MO-SHU-XIONG delivery: The first O2O delivery platform in town, making efforts to enlarge the delivery service including making orders online, on-time deliveries, farm produce, etc in towns especially in Nei Meng Gu.

Promotions,mobile clientand administrationmake up the whole product. Promotions are composed of the official site and its relations, the mobile client can be obtained from App Store and Android stores, administration provides management over stores, goods and finance. We have chosen a cross-platform native development tool - Deviceone as our framework and we used hybrid development, as a result, we implemented all the required functions perfectly under the condition that the user experience is okay and solved all the compatibility problems.

This project required teamworks and remote-coorperation, i did feel a little busy during the process of coordinating different departments, organizing product logics and testing but also very pleased.

INTERNET FINANCIAL PLATFORM

互联网金融大数据监管平台

22nd Jul 2016

数控金融监管平台针对贵阳市大数据建设和金融业发展现状而设计创建，利用数据可视化工具（Echarts）通过图表形式全方位，多方面地展示数据，从而通过大数据技术对个人和公司进行评判，以防范金融风险。使用到的图表包括中国地图（可下钻）、柱状图、饼图、折线图、散点图（可调节）、热力图（动态）、雷达图、堆栈图、柱线图。

除了承担绝大部分的前端开发工作，我还参与了产品形态的改进。轮换过不同岗位、体验过上线前的紧迫、挑战过超越能力范围的任务，才开始懂得自己需要什么、能做什么。

Internet Financial Platformis designed and constructed for the Big Data career and financial business in Guiyang province. It uses the data visualization tool - Echarts to display data in all directions and aspects in the form of various charts, in order to evaluate individuals and enterprises, preventing them from financial risks.Those charts include ChinaMap(digging enabled), bar charts, pie charts, line charts, scatter(adjustable), heat map(dynamic), radar, stack charts, mixed charts.

Apart from all the front-end developement job i have done, i also took apart in the improvement plan of the product. The experience of acting as different roles, the anxious feeling before deadline of the project, the tasks which are beyond my current ability, those precious memories have taught me what i really need and what i really can do.

LYRICS WIKI

歌词维基

1st Jul 2016

这是由Riddle建立并维护的用于展示和分享歌词及翻译内容的网站。随着国内音乐播放器功能和版权保护的日益完善，歌曲的歌词翻译是否耿实美好也愈发得到用户的重视；用户的听歌习惯也随着智能设备的不断发展而改变，对曲库及词库的管理和分类将影响用户听歌质量的好坏；音乐软件的社区化也是目前的主流趋势，因此这是一个：

有维基百科的全面详实、网易云音乐的音乐情怀、Lofter的清新文艺以及魔镜歌词网的内容建设的产品。前期它是静态网页，歌曲会按专辑分类、歌词由人工翻译、少许设计，后期将根据添加的内容进行扩充和推广。

It is a website set up and maintained by riddle which collects and displays lyrics. Users pay more and more attention to lyrics as the music softwares are having more and more powerful functions and knowledge privacy legacies; Management and classification of songs and lyrics can also be a major effector of a user's experience; music softwares are becoming more social at the mean time. Therefore, this's a product which is:

Overall comprehensive as WiKi, passionate as Netease music, fresh as Lofter and constructed as Mojing. Currently this static website only has album classifications, lyrics translations, a little design, it will be enriched and promoted in the future.
