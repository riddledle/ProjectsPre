INTERNET FINANCIAL PLATFORM

Feb 14, 2016

互联网金融大数据监管平台

22nd July 2016——22nd Aug 2016

从0到1建设数控金融监管平台 ，该监管服务平台针对贵阳市大数据建设和金融业发展现状而设计创建，利用数据可视化工具（Echarts）通过图表形式全方位，多方面地展示数据，从而通过大数据技术对个人和公司进行评判，以防范金融风险。

Internet Financial Platformis conducted from 0 to 1,which is designed and constructed for the Big Data career and financial business in Guiyang province. It uses data visualization tool - Echarts to display data in all directions and aspects in form of various charts, in order to evaluate individuals and enterprises and avoid financial risks.
