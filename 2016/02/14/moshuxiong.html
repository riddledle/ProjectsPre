魔术熊手机外卖APP

22nd Aug 2016——30th Oct 2016

从0到1制作魔术熊手机外卖APP，参与产品设计、开发、测试。魔术熊手机外卖APP包含宣传推广页、手机客户端、商家管理后台共三部分，宣传推广页包含官方网站及其相关信息页，手机客户端上架App Store和安卓应用市场，商家管理后台供商户和超级管理员管理商铺、商品和财务。

MO-SHU-XIONG APPis constructed from 0 to 1, my contribution contains product design, development and test. Promotions,mobile clientand administrationmake up the whole product. Promotions is composed of the official site and its relations, the mobile client can be obtained from App Store and Android stores, the administration provides management over stores, goods and finance.
