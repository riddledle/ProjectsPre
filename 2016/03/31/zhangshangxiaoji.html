掌上小暨微信平台

31st Mar 2016

掌上小暨定位于暨南大学，是集校园教学、工作和生活服务功能于一体的线上结构性服务系统平台，其目的在于整合校园各类信息与服务，通过O2O运营模式为暨南大学广大师生提供一个全方位、广覆盖、高效便捷的网络综合服务环境。

掌上小暨自2016年3月创立至今，粉丝人数达13000人，主要活跃于暨南大学南校区，覆盖南校区90%以上学生，最近30天图文阅读总次数达244701次，累计190272人阅读，南校区自媒体平台第一。

我因为契机加入了该平台并伴随其成长发展，期间参与过商业合作、活动运营和内容运营，为平台积累了大量用户。校园自媒体的运营经历对我来说是大学期间一次不错的体验。

InMyJnuis located in JNU which is an online service platform integrating teaching affairs and life service in the campus. It aims at providing a wide-spread comprehensive internet environment for teachers and students by running the O2O operating model.

The platform has gained over 13000 fans since its foundation in 2016.3 covering over 90% of the students in south campus, the total views in the latest month has achieved 244701 times by 190272 people, winning the first place in south campus.

I became part of the operating team by chance. I've taken part in its commercial cooperations, event operations and content operations and won many fans for the platform. This is genuinely a meaningful experience for me during my campus life.
