魔术熊

22nd Aug 2016

魔术熊外送：县城第一家O2O外送平台，致力于发展三四线城市移动互联网外送服务，提供网上订餐、准时外送、农产品、土特产交易；以内蒙古五个县城为试点，正在积极扩张。

产品的全部内容包含宣传推广页、手机客户端、商家管理后台共三部分，宣传推广页包含官方网站及其相关信息页，手机客户端上架App Store和安卓应用市场，商家管理后台供商户和超级管理员管理商铺、商品和财务。

这个项目采用的是团队协作开发和远程合作的方式，尽管协调各部门、梳理产品逻辑、产品测试与上线等事情让我感到了忙碌但同时我也非常开心。

MO-SHU-XIONG delivery: The first O2O delivery platform in town, making efforts to enlarge the delivery service including making orders online, on-time deliveries, farm produce, etc in towns especially in Nei Meng Gu.

Promotions,mobile clientand administrationmake up the whole product. Promotions are composed of the official site and its relations, the mobile client can be obtained from App Store and Android stores, administration provides management over stores, goods and finance.

This project required teamworks and remote-coorperation, i did feel a little busy during the process of coordinating different departments, organizing product logics and testing but also very pleased.
