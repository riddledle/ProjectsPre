INTERNET FINANCIAL PLATFORM

Jul 22, 2016

互联网金融大数据监管平台

22nd Jul 2016

数控金融监管平台针对贵阳市大数据建设和金融业发展现状而设计创建，利用数据可视化工具（Echarts）通过图表形式全方位，多方面地展示数据，从而通过大数据技术对个人和公司进行评判，以防范金融风险。

这是我的第一个项目，通宵的情形还历历在目，但是能够从0到1完成一个项目的感受非常美妙，Echarts也是一款表现力很强的数据工具，这次经历使我深深被人类的潜力所感动，那是无限的东西，感受非常深刻。

Internet Financial Platformis designed and constructed for the Big Data career and financial business in Guiyang province. It uses the data visualization tool - Echarts to display data in all directions and aspects in the form of various charts, in order to evaluate individuals and enterprises, preventing them from financial risks.

This is my very first project ever in my career, there were days when i stayed up all night. However the fantastic taste of experiencing a 0 to 1 project is rewarding.Echarts is also worth learning. I can never forget how i was impressed by the infinite potential of a simple human being for the rest of my life.
