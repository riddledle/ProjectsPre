歌词维基

1st Jul 2016

这是Riddle建立并维护的，灵感来源于WiKi、网易云音乐以及魔镜歌词网等站点。作为静态网页，内容建设包含了分类、翻译、排版。尽管并不包含什么技术含量，但是从产品的角度来说，它能反映用户（也就是我，笑）的真实需要，包含了我对它的许多倾注和热爱，相信我会维护下去。

It was set up and maintained by Riddle, this idea was intrigued by Wiki,NetEase music,MoJing lyricsetc. As a static site, its constructions involve classification,translation and typesetting.It's not high-tech, nevertheless, in the view of a product, it can reflect the need of the user(me :) ) and it carries my devotion to it.Somehow i feel like i'm gonna keep close to it.
