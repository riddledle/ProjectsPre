Jul 1, 2016

歌词维基

1st Jul 2016

这是由Riddle建立并维护的用于展示和分享歌词及翻译内容的网站。随着国内音乐播放器功能和版权保护的日益完善，歌曲的歌词翻译是否耿实美好也愈发得到用户的重视；用户的听歌习惯也随着智能设备的不断发展而改变，对曲库及词库的管理和分类将影响用户听歌质量的好坏；音乐软件的社区化也是目前的主流趋势，因此这是一个：

有维基百科的全面详实、网易云音乐的音乐情怀、Lofter的清新文艺以及魔镜歌词网的内容建设的产品。前期它是静态网页，歌曲会按专辑分类、歌词由人工翻译、少许设计，后期将根据添加的内容进行扩充和推广。

It is a website set up and maintained by riddle which collects and displays lyrics. Users pay more and more attention to lyrics as the music softwares are having more and more powerful functions and knowledge privacy legacies; Management and classification of songs and lyrics can also be a major effector of a user's experience; music softwares are becoming more social at the mean time. Therefore, this's a product which is:

Overall comprehensive as WiKi, passionate as Netease music, fresh as Lofter and constructed as Mojing. Currently this static website only has album classifications, lyrics translations, a little design, it will be enriched and promoted in the future.
