华人说

1st Nov 2016

华人说是一款针对海外华人的新闻社区类App，旨在打造全球华人聚集的资讯平台。在产品设计上，它借鉴了海外社区新闻类产品－Reddit的官方App，内容展示的方式则会更加符合国人的阅读习惯。这是一个利用敏捷开发模式快速上线试错的产品，以期达到抢占海外市场的目的。

这款产品能够诞生，前期我们做了许多准备工作，包括分析Reddit并拆分功能、选择合适的技术框架、规划产品的发展战略、通过内部投票的方式挑选产品名称与口号等。

通过不断推动一个想法，使之从空中聚焦和落地，并且做到快速上线，我觉得是很刺激的体验。

HUA REN SHUOis a PGC App for Chinese oversea which is aimed at becoming an information platform gathering global Chinese. It gained design principles from Reddit but information is displayed in a way which is more familiar to Chinese. This is an agile product for trials and errors expecting to domain the oversea market.

We made lots of preparations for this product during its prophase including researching Reddit and its functions, choosing correct frameworks, designing product and picking its name&slogan by voting.

To some degree, it's exciting to promote an idea and bring it to reality.
