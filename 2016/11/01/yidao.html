易道网球

1st Nov 2016

易道网球是一款主营健身教练课程和运动场地预订服务的运动健身产品。它以微信公众号为载体，将目前易道体育线下运营的会员套餐系统线上化，让用户可在微信上进行会员套餐购买、课程预约、课程管理、上课签到等行为，运营人员可在运营后台管理课程及会员，从而提高易道体育目前的运营效率及营收。

我参与了易道的初期版本的迭代。当时也是要写很多文档，写产品需求和竞品分析，那时候困难的不是理产品逻辑，而是怎么兼顾工作和其它事情。

Yidao tennisis a WeChat project which serves the sports&fitness market and offers reservations for teaching courses and venues. It turns the VIP system from off-line to on-line, making the prachase for VIP service and reservation for courses conducted in WeiXin possible, administrators can also perform admistrations over users resulting in an improvement in efficiency.

I was the product assistant and took part in its sprints during the prophase. I was required to write many documents of course like PRD, competitive analysis. It's the confusion of making work and other routines compatible that troubled me rather than the process of figuring out product logics.
